## 1. Lecture Summary

### 1.1 What Utilitarianism Claims  
Utilitarianism asserts that the morally right action is the one that maximizes total happiness.  
Bentham defined happiness as pleasure and the absence of pain, and believed all pleasures are equal in value if they produce the same quantity.

### 1.2 Mill’s Correction: Higher vs. Lower Pleasures  
Mill argued that human mental, moral, and imaginative capacities create **higher pleasures**  
(e.g., Shakespeare, philosophy, creativity).  
Lower pleasures are sensory, easily obtained, and short-lived (e.g., extreme sports thrills, The Simpsons).

Mill:  
> “Better to be a human being dissatisfied than a pig satisfied.”

2. My Commentary
2.1 Can Value, Transactions, and Life Be Priced?
Human beings engage in transactions to satisfy needs, from bartering to monetary systems, which made value quantifiable.
But whether life and dignity can be quantified is another issue.

Democratic countries:
Human rights and dignity are valued; utilitarianism is constrained by law and morality.

Authoritarian countries:
People are treated as state property; organs can be priced, and children can be bought and sold.
Utilitarianism degenerates into pure profit calculation, eroding all boundaries of humanity and morality.

2.2 My Understanding of the Core Issue
The mentality of "mocking the poor but not the prostitute" is a result of human nature being marketized.
When everything in society has a price tag, it means people are no longer the end, but become the means.

Requires mental activity, imagination, emotional depth, and cultural understanding.

3.2 Extreme Sports → Intense but lower-level happiness

Where dignity is absent, utilitarianism turns into:

## 1. 課堂摘要

### 1.1 功利主義的主張  
功利主義主張，道德上正確的行為是能夠最大化整體幸福的行為。  
邊沁認為幸福是快樂與痛苦的缺席，並認為若產生的快樂量相同，所有快樂的價值都是相等的。

### 1.2 密爾的修正：高級與低級快樂  
密爾主張人類的智力、道德與想像力能力創造了**高級快樂**  
（如：莎士比亞、哲學、創造力）。  
低級快樂則是感官的、容易獲得且短暫的（如：極限運動刺激、《辛普森家庭》）。

密爾：  
>「寧願做一個不滿足的人類，也不要做一頭滿足的豬。」

## 2. My Commentary (中文)

### 2.1 價值、交易、生命是否能標價  
人類因需求產生交易，從以物易物到金錢制度，價值開始量化。  
但生命與尊嚴是否能量化，是另一個問題。

民主國家：  
重視人的權利與尊嚴，功利主義受到法律與道德的約束。

專制國家：  
人民被視為國家財產，器官可以定價，小孩可以買賣。  
功利主義墮落成純粹利益計算，人性與道德底線完全被侵蝕。

### 2.2 我對核心問題的理解  
「笑貧不笑娼」式的價值觀，是人性被市場化後的結果。  
當社會的每件事都能標價，就代表人已經不再是目的，而變成手段。
