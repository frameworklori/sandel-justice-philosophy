 Justice Lecture 5 — Conscription, Patriotism, and the Right to Life

（English Version）

I. Why Do Governments Claim the Moral Authority to Draft Citizens?

Governments often justify conscription by appealing to political authority:
the state protects citizens’ rights, and in return, citizens owe civic duties.

But Locke argues that life is an inalienable right.
If so, how can a government force citizens into situations where death is possible?

This creates a fundamental paradox:
to protect life, the state may require the risking of life.

⸻

II. Does Conscription Violate the Right to Life?

Sandel presents three models of military recruitment:
	1.	Mandatory Draft (Lottery System)
	•	Everyone is equally eligible to serve.
	•	But forcing someone into lethal danger may violate their basic right to life.
	2.	Volunteer Army (High Pay & Benefits)
	•	Appears voluntary but is shaped by economic inequality.
	•	The poor “choose” to enlist due to lack of alternatives.
	•	This is not genuine freedom but coerced choice under unequal conditions.
	3.	Privatized Military (Contractors / Mercenaries)
	•	Life is literally priced.
	•	Wealthy citizens avoid service while others sell their risk.

⸻

III. The Carnegie Substitution Example

When wealthy individuals can pay substitutes to go to war, society becomes morally distorted:
	•	Life becomes a market commodity.
	•	Obligations become transferable by money.
	•	The burdens of war fall disproportionately on the lower classes.

This undermines the idea of equal civic obligation.

⸻

IV. Who Fought in the Iraq War?

Sandel points to data showing that soldiers who died in Iraq disproportionately came from:
	•	Low-income families
	•	Rural areas
	•	Minoritized communities

Thus, even a “volunteer army” reflects structural inequality.
War sacrifices become class-based, not equal.

⸻

V. Patriotism vs. Market Incentives

Two main motivations for military service:
	1.	Patriotism
	•	A sense of belonging and collective duty.
	2.	Financial Incentives
	•	Higher pay for higher risk.

But if patriotism is shaped through social pressure or ideological training,
is it still voluntary?
And if service is only driven by money,
is it morally legitimate?

Neither model is morally stable without a fair system behind it.

⸻

VI. Where Does Political Obligation Come From?

Sandel presents three theories:
	1.	Consent
	•	Locke: Government authority comes from citizens’ consent.
	•	Problem: Most people never explicitly consent.
	2.	Civic Duty
	•	Citizenship carries inherent responsibilities.
	•	But what justifies this duty?
	3.	Identity & Patriotism
	•	People feel obligated because they belong to the nation.
	•	Danger: identity-based manipulation or propaganda.

⸻

VII. Justice Requires a Complete and Fair Institutional Structure

For civic obligations—like military service—to be legitimate,
the system must be:
	•	fair in opportunity
	•	equal in burden-sharing
	•	resistant to exploitation
	•	not purchasable by wealth
	•	grounded in a just and defensive purpose, not aggression

Without this complete structural fairness,
obligations become oppression, not duty.

⸻

✦ LORI VIEW

A Civilized Duty Requires a Complete Institutional Circle

From the LORI perspective, rights and duties must form a closed and complete institutional circle. If citizens enjoy rights but the system fails to distribute duties fairly, the political structure collapses into inequality, coercion, and moral distortion.

A just system must ensure: • Equal starting conditions • A tax structure that prevents extreme wealth concentration • Duties that cannot be bought, substituted, or outsourced • Military service that reflects shared responsibility rather than class sacrifice • War that is defensive and morally legitimate—not aggressive • Patriotism based on free identification, not indoctrination

Only when the institutional design is whole and fair can obligations truly become civic duties, not involuntary burdens.

A broken system produces soldiers by coercion. A complete system produces citizens by dignity.

✦ 正義論第5講：徵兵制 × 愛國義務 × 生命權

（中文版本）

I. 為什麼政府可以主張徵兵？

政府常說：
“我保護你的權利，所以你也必須履行義務。”

但依照洛克：
生命權不可被剝奪。

如果如此，政府怎麼能強迫人民冒生命危險？

這形成了重要的矛盾：
為了保護生命，政府卻可能要求人民犧牲生命。

⸻

II. 徵兵是否侵犯生命權？

Sandel 列出三種徵兵方式：
	1.	強制徵兵（抽籤）
	•	人人都有可能被徵召
	•	但強迫人民冒生命危險＝侵犯生命權
	2.	志願兵（高薪、高福利）
	•	表面自由
	•	但弱勢者因生活壓力被“迫”選擇
	•	是不平等造成的偽自由
	3.	軍事外包（雇佣兵）
	•	生命被市場標價
	•	富人可以花錢逃避義務
	•	戰爭更階級化

⸻

III. 卡內基買替身的例子

當有錢人可以花錢讓別人代替當兵：
	•	生命變成商品
	•	義務可以用金錢轉讓
	•	犧牲永遠由底層承擔

這破壞了公民義務的平等性。

⸻

IV. 伊拉克戰爭：誰犧牲最多？

統計顯示：
	•	多為低收入家庭
	•	多為鄉村地區青年
	•	多為少數族裔

看似自願，其實是結構性的不平等壓迫。

⸻

V. 愛國 vs. 高薪：哪個更正當？

兩種動機：
	1.	愛國：身份認同
	2.	薪資誘因：金錢驅動

但問題是：
	•	若愛國是洗腦來的，是否還算自由？
	•	若當兵是因金錢誘惑，是否還算道德？
	•	都需要公平制度作為基礎，否則都會變質

⸻

VI. 政治義務的來源？
	1.	同意（洛克）
但大多數人從未真正“同意”政府。
	2.	公民義務
但什麼義務算合理？
	3.	身份與愛國情感
容易被操控或利用。

⸻

VII. 公平制度是義務正當性的唯一基礎

只有在制度完整且公平時：
	•	義務才是真正的義務
	•	犧牲才具正當性
	•	參軍才是自由的選擇
	•	不是被迫、不是用錢買斷、不是階級壓迫

否則：
義務會變成壓迫，不是道德責任。

⸻

LORI觀點（中文）

在 LORI 的政治哲學中，
權利與義務必須形成完整的制度閉環。
人民享受權利，但制度若無法公平分攤義務，
整個國家就會走向：
	•	不平等
	•	被迫選擇
	•	階級壓迫
	•	義務買賣化
	•	道德失衡

一個正義的制度必須包含：
	•	平等起點
	•	能抑制貧富差距的稅制
	•	義務不可被金錢買斷或替代
	•	兵役的負擔真正全民共享
	•	戰爭目的必須是正當防衛
	•	愛國心必需來自自由認同而非洗腦

制度完整，義務才會成為公民責任；
制度破碎，義務就會變成壓迫。

完整制度生產公民；破碎制度生產犧牲者。

✦ Lori’s Model: A Hybrid Military System for Justice and Effectiveness

(English Version)

I. Why a Hybrid System Is Preferable

A purely mandatory draft violates autonomy and risks sacrificing inexperienced citizens.
A purely volunteer army relies too heavily on economic inequality.
A fully outsourced military commodifies life and disconnects war from society.

A hybrid system—volunteer soldiers + professional military contractors—
balances fairness, expertise, and survival probability.

⸻

II. Professional Contractors Reduce Casualties

Professional military contractors often have:
	•	prior combat experience
	•	survival and tactical expertise
	•	rapid adaptation to foreign environments
	•	familiarity with weapons and high-risk operations

This reduces unnecessary deaths that occur when inexperienced draftees
(face danger without adequate training or real-world exposure).

⸻

III. Volunteers Remain Essential but Should Not Bear Full Risk

Volunteers provide:
	•	civic participation
	•	national identity
	•	stabilizing moral legitimacy

But volunteers may lack the tactical skills of career fighters.
A hybrid system prevents them from becoming the majority of frontline casualties.

⸻

IV. Why Not Mandatory Conscription?

Mandatory draftees:
	•	lack training
	•	lack combat instincts
	•	lack survival experience
	•	become statistically more likely to die in frontline service

This creates moral distortions:
citizens with no expertise bear the highest risk simply because they were born into a draft system.

A just system should not rely on “sacrifice by randomness.”

⸻

V. The Moral Logic of Lori’s Hybrid Model

A hybrid model distributes risk rationally:
	•	Highly trained professionals handle the most dangerous operations
	•	Volunteers serve by genuine choice, not economic coercion
	•	Society avoids random sacrifice
	•	The state avoids relying on poverty-driven enlistment
	•	War remains connected to democratic accountability, not outsourced entirely

This system respects both life and liberty while maintaining national defense.

⸻

✦ Lori’s Hybrid System — 中文版

I. 為什麼混合制更合理？

單一制度都有重大缺陷：
	•	全面徵兵 → 犧牲大量沒有經驗的普通人
	•	純志願兵 → 容易變成窮人賣命、富人安全
	•	純外包雇傭兵 → 生命變成商品、戰爭脫離人民控制

但 「志願兵＋專業外包軍事人員」的混合制
可以同時兼顧「公平性、實際性、生存率」。

⸻

II. 專業軍事外包可以降低死亡率

外包軍事專業者通常具備：
	•	實戰經驗
	•	槍械操作熟練
	•	在外地求生能力高
	•	對高風險任務有真正的經驗

他們比一般志願兵或徵兵制新兵
更能有效生存、避免不必要的犧牲。

⸻

III. 志願兵仍然重要但不應成為主力犧牲者

志願兵代表：
	•	公民參與
	•	保家衛國的道德正當性
	•	對國家的自由承諾

但他們並非每個人都適合承擔高風險作戰任務。
混合制可以避免志願兵變成最大的死亡來源。

⸻

IV. 為何不採全面徵兵？

全面徵兵（尤其抽籤制）會讓：
	•	沒經驗的人上戰場
	•	沒學過武器的人當第一線
	•	不熟悉外地環境的人暴露極高風險

這等於把許多公民送去死亡，而不是建立一支穩定有效的軍隊。

「隨機被抽到 → 上戰場死亡」
本身就是制度不正義。

⸻

V. Lori 混合制的道德邏輯

你的模式建立在：
	•	專業降低死亡率
	•	志願確保政治與道德正當性
	•	避免窮人承擔全部犧牲
	•	避免完全市場化的軍隊失控
	•	避免全面徵兵造成的隨機犧牲

混合制的核心精神是：

戰爭的風險應由最能處理風險的人承擔，
而不是由最弱勢或最隨機的人承擔。

⸻

✦ LORI VIEW — 強化版哲學結論

(English)

A just military system must protect both life and equality.
The hybrid model aligns with this principle:
	•	Expertise protects life
	•	Volunteerism preserves freedom
	•	Fair distribution of risk prevents class-based sacrifice
	•	Democratic oversight prevents moral detachment from war

A hybrid system does not eliminate sacrifice—
it rationalizes it and humanizes it.

⸻

(中文)

一個正義的軍事制度必須同時守護「生命」與「平等」。

混合制的核心精神：
	•	用專業降低死亡
	•	用志願維持自由與道德正當性
	•	避免階級犧牲
	•	避免戰爭脫離民主控制

混合制不是消除犧牲，而是 讓犧牲變得有理由、有正義性，而不是盲目與隨機。
